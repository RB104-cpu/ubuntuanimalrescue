About Us

Who We Are

Ubuntu Animal Rescue is a non-profit organization based in Pretoria. We are dedicated to rescuing abandoned, abused, and stray animals, providing them with care, and finding them loving homes.

Our Values

  • Compassion for all living beings
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Community-driven rescue efforts
  • Promoting responsible pet ownership
