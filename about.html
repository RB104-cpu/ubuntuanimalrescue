About Us

Ubuntu Animal Rescue is dedicated to saving, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned animals. We believe in the spirit of community and compassion, ensuring every animal has the chance for a loving home.

Ubuntu Animal Rescue was founded in 2015 in Gauteng with a simple vision: to give abandoned and abused animals a second chance at life. What started as a small community initiative has grown into a recognized rescue organization with volunteers, adoption programs, and community outreach.

Our Mission

To provide safe shelter, medical care, and loving homes for abandoned animals. We believe every animal deserves a chance to be valued and cared for.

Our Vision

A future where every animal in our community is safe, loved, and part of a family.

What We Do

  • Rescue and shelter stray and abandoned animals.
  • Provide medical care including vaccinations and sterilization.
  • Host adoption events and find loving homes for animals.
  • Run educational programs on responsible pet ownership.

Our Impact

Since 2015, we have rescued over 1,200 animals, successfully rehomed more than 850 pets, and engaged 200+ volunteers in our programs.
